# Getting Started with GoAds

Welcome to the GoAds documentation. This guide will help you get started with your agency advertising account.

## Quick Start

Follow these steps to begin advertising with your GoAds account:

1. **Choose Your Platform**: Select Facebook, Google Ads, or TikTok
2. **Select a Plan**: Pick a pricing plan that fits your needs
3. **Complete Setup**: Provide necessary information for account creation
4. **Start Advertising**: Begin running campaigns within 24-48 hours

## Account Types

### Facebook Agency Accounts

Perfect for social media marketers running Facebook and Instagram campaigns. Features include:

- Higher spending limits
- Better account stability
- Business Manager integration
- Dedicated support

### Google Ads Agency Accounts

Ideal for search and display advertising across Google's network:

- Access to all Google Ads features
- Enhanced billing options
- Manager account support
- Performance optimization tools

### TikTok Agency Accounts

Great for brands targeting younger audiences on TikTok:

- TikTok Ads Manager access
- Creative tools and resources
- Advanced targeting options
- Analytics and reporting
